A note from the developer.

I'm a reader who decided to build the tool I couldn't find. I was tired of reading apps that felt like digital chores or social networks. I wanted something that respected the quiet nature of a book.

Why I built this.

We live in an era of infinite scrolls. I found myself losing the ability to sit with a book for an hour without checking my phone. I built Trace to be a calm, aesthetic companion to help me stay consistent.

Core Utility

Pick up where you left off

Your library shows active books with real-time progress. One tap takes you straight into a session—no friction, no noise.

Sessions that hold focus

A distraction-free interface that tracks your time or page goals. It’s designed to be a quiet observer, not a distraction.

Log what matters

Record your pages and your thoughts. Scan passages directly from the physical book to keep your favorite quotes in one place.

See your reading life clearly

Pace, history, and projected finish dates—all derived from your real behavior, keeping the finish line in sight.
