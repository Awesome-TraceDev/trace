A note from the developer.

I'm a reader who decided to build the tool I couldn't find. I was tired of reading apps that felt like digital chores or social networks designed to sell my data. I wanted a quiet, intentional space that respected the act of reading.

Trace is my attempt at that, a privacy-first companion built to help me (and hopefully you) reclaim the horizon and see every book through to the final page.
